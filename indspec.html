Banking and Financial Institutions

Bridgelake Associates provides data management and analytic tools for financial institutions in Ghana to integrate and analyze data sources. Data sources from customer relationship management(CRM) systems, trading systems, operational logs, network logs etc. can be analyzed and essential insights provided. Information received by customers from various ATMs, online transactions can be used to create insights into how the business is performing. With the advent of a cashless economy in Ghana, now is the time for financial institutions to switch to big data analytics. We save financial institutions time and money used to analyze their legacy data systems. With compliance regulations on the increase, our big data analytics capabilities will provide timely, accurate, controlled and comprehensive analysis, keeping up with regulatory institutions needs. Our big data analytics solution for financial institutions can be used for marketing automation, Customer Relationship Management as well as business strategies.

Retail

We offer retail companies in the burgeoning shopping industry in Ghana a competitive advantage to knowing their customers. We provide a means to better understand shoppers purchases both online and their interactions with your brand on social media. Our analytics provides retail companies with a strong customer relationship management strategy. By using our social sentiment data strategy we are able to help monitor how shoppers are responding to a company’s offer. With the rise of the use of mobile, tablets and social media, we are able to provide analysis on customer mobile usage, social interaction, call center behavior etc. Bridgelake Associates will provide insights on transaction data, foot traffic and in-store checkout wait time which can be used in marketing strategies. We help retailers personalize their marketing and sales information.

Travel and Hospitality

After the liberalization of the airline industry in Ghana and across Africa, there has been an increase in accessibility, availability and affordability to air flights. These increase in flights provides lots of opportunities to the airline companies as well as some challenges. Our custom data analytics solution helps airlines study customer trends and behavior that can be used for corporate strategies. Our solutions can help you track bags, personalize offers, increase loyalty programs and optimize the operations of the airline. The ever competitive hotel industry in Ghana can benefit tremendously with big data analytics. Our solutions help hotels exceed guest expectations. We help improve the quality of the customer journey. We help hotels increase their customization to customers, improve relationships with tour agencies and also their restaurants to predict menu items that customers will likely order. We also help hotels predict room occupancy rate thereby improving sales and marketing.

Government Institutions

Government agencies in Ghana face enormous challenge with using data to formulate policies. Most agencies do not have the means to collect, analyze and visualize data. Bridgelake Associates works with the various Ministries, Departments and Agencies (MDA) as well as the various Metropolitan, Municipal and District Assemblies (MMDA). We can help the government analyze large set of data to improve national security. Our solutions can help national security to track data and prevent fraud. We can help the tax organizations analyze both structured and unstructured data to identify discrepancies and multiple identities and also to prevent tax fraud. We can help the statistical department collect and analyze their data and make accurate projections. The various identification departments can benefit from our big data analytic capabilities.
