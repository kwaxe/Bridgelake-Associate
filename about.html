Company Mission

Company Vision

Bridgleake Associates ensures companies make sense of their valuable data by providing business intelligence and analytics which increases their competitive advantage in the marketplace.

Our Values


  • Customer Centricity: We will work to exceed our customer’s expectations by understanding and developing well meaning relationships that lead to profitability and satisfaction.

  • Value Creation: We will provide long-term economic benefits for our customers by helping them optimize their operations with analytical tools

  • Integrity: Business with our customers will be conducted with integrity. Data obtained from customers will be kept confidential and all privacy arrangements will be respected

  • Trust: Bridgelake Associates core value is building trust with its clients. Customer engagements will be conducted with trust and respect to create a strong partnership.

Bridgelake Associates has core values that reflects in the culture and principles of the company. The company ensures thes e values are adhered to by it’s employees. These shared values have created standards, code of conduct and practices.
